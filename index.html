S.U.N.C.E

(pron.sun-see)

VISION :

Provide quality need based services for persons' with disability also known as People Abled Differently(PAD)

mission :

Empower persons living with disability,through special services that enable them perform their daily function in an ordinary way like other people.

OBJECTIVES :

  • Provide assistive and adaptive technologies for life long learning and integration into mainstream living.
  • Produce learning (reading) materials for visually impaired people.
  • Facilitate information access through adaptive technology.
  • Connect people living with disability to their source of information.
  • Provide a social and consultative platform of awareness and advocacy.
  • To facilitate training for integrated life skills
  • Provide access for global information through the use of internet.
